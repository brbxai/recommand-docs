## What it does (high level)

The integration connects ERPNext’s e-document flow with Recommand so you can:

- **Send e-documents**: transmit Peppol invoices and credit notes from ERPNext.
- **Receive e-documents**: ingest incoming documents via webhooks and/or polling.
- **Automatically process documents**: create ERPNext EDocument records and continue your normal ERPNext workflow.
